About APD Painting Services

APD Painting Services has been a trusted choice for homeowners across Chicagoland since 1994. Owner Anthony "Tony" Portnoy brings hands-on expertise, efficiency and personal commitment to every project.

A native of the north suburbs, being the only child of an artist father and interior decorator mother gives him a unique perspective on color and design. Although he has a background in marketing and communications, painting has been a constant his entire adult life.

Tony works primarily in the North Shore and Northwest suburbs, gravitating toward Benjamin Moore and Sherwin Williams products. While tackling mostly residential work, he occasionally takes on unique or commercial projects.

Skilled, fully insured and known for fair, honest pricing, he treats every home as if it were his own.

With over 32 years of experience, Tony has built a loyal clientele through fair pricing and quality work that speaks for itself.

"Moving in, moving out or staying in place all deserve a great paint job" - Tony Portnoy

The APD Approach

Our Services

Quality painting solutions for Chicagoland homeowners

Interior Painting

Full-room transformations with premium paints, expert cutting and invisible seams. From a single accent wall to a whole-home refresh.

Exterior Painting

Weather-ready coatings that protect beautify and boost curb appeal. Power washing, scraping, sanding and priming provide lasting finishes built to withstand Chicago seasons.

Cabinet & Trim Refinishing

Breathe new life into kitchens, bathroom vanities and built-ins; professional results at a fraction of the replacement cost. Spray-quality finishes on cabinetry; Refresh or refinish doors, windows, casings, crown molding, baseboards and architectural detail.

Color Consultation

Professional guidance on palette selection tailored to your home's architecture, purpose and personal style. Find the perfect shade with confidence!

Decks, Fences & Playsets

Washing, staining or sealing helps restore, protect and maintain outdoor wood spaces, including Rainbow Play Systems.

Drywall Repair

Water damage from plumbing leaks or bumps and bruises on walls from everyday living are common issues. Patched, primed and painted to blend seamlessly with your existing walls.
